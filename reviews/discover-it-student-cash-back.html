Discover it® Student Cash Back Review: Training Wheels for Your Wallet

For college students who want to build credit without blowing it all on late-night pizza, the Discover it® Student Cash Back is basically a starter pack for adulthood. This is one of the few things in college you won’t regret in five years.

Perks That (Actually) Matter

  • 5% cash back on rotating categories (Amazon, gas, restaurants, etc.), so you can pretend you understand “budgeting.”
  • 1% back on everything else—so you always get something, even if you mess up the calendar.
  • Cashback Match: Discover doubles all your cash back the first year. Yes, really. No math degree required.
  • No annual fee—you spent it all on tuition anyway.
  • Good Grade Reward: $20 statement credit for GPAs over 3.0. If only passing stats paid this well.

Some Bummer Trade-Offs

  • Rotating categories—don’t forget to activate or you’ll miss out. (Reminder: adulting includes “chores.”)
  • Discover not accepted everywhere—your favorite taco truck might just say “nah.”
  • No travel perks, fancy nonsense, or airline lounges. You’re a student, not a CEO.

Who Needs This?

Students and young adults who want rewards, a credit score that doesn’t make loan officers weep, and simple terms. If “rotating category” sounds like too much effort, look elsewhere.

The Bottom Line

The Discover it® Student Cash Back is for people ready to start adulting, one month at a time. Your future self will thank you; your taco truck, maybe not.

*Not funded by Discover—sadly. Read those rotating categories, live your best college life, and maybe stop spending cash back on vending machine burritos.
