Venture X vs. The Competition

Venture X vs. Chase Sapphire Reserve

Reserve costs $550 ($250 net after $300 credit), earns 3x travel/dining, has better transfer partners. Venture X costs $5 net, earns 10x on hotels, has simpler redemption. Winner: Venture X for value, Reserve for transfer options.

Venture X vs. Amex Platinum

Platinum costs $695 ($100+ net after credits), earns 5x flights/hotels, has Centurion lounges. Venture X costs $5 net, has simpler credits, better everyday earning (2x vs 1x). Winner: Venture X unless you need Centurion lounges or fly Delta constantly.

Who Should Get This Card

Perfect for:

  • Travelers who book hotels through portals anyway (10x is insane)
  • Anyone who values simplicity (2x on everything, no categories)
  • People upgrading from Chase Sapphire Preferred

Skip it if:

  • You travel 0-2 times per year (get the regular Venture instead)
  • You need Centurion Lounge access specifically (Amex only)
  • You're deep in the Chase ecosystem and love Ultimate Rewards
  • You won't use the $300 travel credit (unlikely, but possible)

Bottom Line: Is Capital One Venture X Worth It?

Skip it if: You travel less than 3 times per year or never book hotels.
